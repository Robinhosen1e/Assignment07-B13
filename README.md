# KenKeeper

## Live Link: https://assignment-b07.vercel.app

## Overview
KenKeeper is a web app designed to make staying connected feel intentional rather than accidental. Instead of letting friendships drift, it keeps a subtle record of your last interactions with each person—whether it was a call, message, or video chat—and turns that into clear, visual insights. With an easy-to-read timeline and analytics, you can quickly see who you haven’t reached out to in a while and keep your relationships active without the mental effort of remembering everything.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Individual Friend Profiles
Access a dedicated page for each friend by clicking Details, where all relevant information is neatly organized in one place.

### 2. Intelligent Interaction Logging
Every interaction—whether a call, message, or video chat—is automatically recorded, giving you a clear history of your communication over time.

### 3. Data-Driven Insights with Charts
Interaction data is presented through visual charts, helping you quickly understand communication patterns and frequency at a glance.
